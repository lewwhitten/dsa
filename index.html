🧭 Today’s Lesson: Linear Transformations

Unit 3.9 – Transformations of Graphs of Linear Functions

Learning Intention:
I am learning how changes to an equation affect the position and direction of its graph.
I can describe and graph translations (shifts) and reflections of the parent function y = x.

Success Criteria:
✅ I can identify the parent function and describe its family.
✅ I can explain what happens when numbers are added or subtracted inside or outside the parentheses.
✅ I can recognize reflections over the x-axis and y-axis.
✅ I can write and graph new equations to match each transformation.
